The Basics

What Is Valet Trash?

Valet trash is a doorstep waste collection service offered to multifamily apartment communities. Instead of residents carrying their bags down hallways, stairs, and parking lots to a distant dumpster, a uniformed valet collects bags directly from outside each unit door — typically five nights a week.

Unlike a gym or pool that only some residents use, every single resident generates trash — making valet waste the one amenity with universal daily value. Property managers across the country consistently rank it the #1 amenity residents say they can't live without.

For properties, it's also a direct revenue opportunity. Properties collect a per-door service fee from residents, pay the valet provider, and keep the margin — turning what used to be a headache into a monthly profit center.

The Process

Seamless 3-Step Process

Luxury property sanitation simple enough to run on autopilot five nights a week.

1

Doorstep Placement

Residents place bagged, tied waste in their provided 13-gallon leak-proof containers outside their doors by 8 PM.

2

Professional Collection

Uniformed, background-checked valets collect waste from every unit overnight, Sunday–Thursday.

3

Direct-to-Dumpster Disposal

Valets transport waste to on-site compactors using leak-proof carts, leaving hallways immaculate every night.
