The Basics

What Is Valet Trash?

Valet trash is a doorstep waste collection service offered to multifamily apartment communities. Instead of residents carrying trash to distant dumpsters, our professional teams collect it nightly from their front doors.

Unlike a gym or pool that only some residents use, every single resident generates trash — making valet waste the one amenity with 100% participation and adoption.

For properties, it is also a direct revenue opportunity. Properties collect a predictable per-door service fee from residents while optimizing their operational expenses.
